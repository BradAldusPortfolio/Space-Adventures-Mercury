## Space-Adventures-Mercury

🚀 Space Adventures is the very first game I designed and published under my studio, Simian Studios LTD.

🌌 This exciting platformer series takes players on an unforgettable journey across the solar system, with each game exploring a brand-new planet filled with unique environments, creative mechanics, and fresh challenges.

🪐 Every instalment features 30 action-packed levels where players must overcome tricky obstacles, adapt to changing physics, and expect the unexpected as they uncover the mysteries of space.

🎮 I also had the amazing opportunity to work alongside Xbox to bring Space Adventures to their systems, making the game available for players on console to enjoy.

✨ No two planets — or levels — are ever the same, making every adventure feel fresh, fun, and full of surprises!
